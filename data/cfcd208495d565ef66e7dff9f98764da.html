Background Story of Bob

Bob,who had made his living by dancing for a long time, at length escaped from his master.and returned to his former companions in the woods.Bob was born in 2010, who was too young to go back home by himself.

On the way to home, many monsters were willing to catch Bob. Bob had to escape from monsters' claws. Else, Bob would be the dinner of monsters!

The game called Bob Go Home was coded in Javascript & Canvas. The game used simple cartoon images and smooth background music. Using direction key to control hero's steps. Don't touch any monsters in the map.

Do you have a jet brain in designing games? Do you have a gift in drawing picture? Welcome to join us.
